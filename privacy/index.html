Privacy Policy

Plant Pantry stores pantry and freshness information for app functionality. Local notifications are used only for user-requested freshness reminders. No tracking, advertising SDK, or third-party analytics SDK is included in the current local app build.

No tracking, advertising SDK, tracking pixels, link shorteners, or third-party analytics are included in the current local app build unless added later with an explicit update to this policy.

Website analytics
